        SISTER’S SMILE (click!)
        10.12.2022
      

      
a poem published online in Double Yolk! 

        
        
          this stanza is still...strangely cool:

          
          I see her friends mindlessly nod
          At the vivid details of her recent travels to a city
          Under lockdown. They praise her volunteerism
          At the dilapidated soup kitchen she’s never visited.
          

  
          it sort of reminds me of this part of a Taylor Swift song, seven:
  
          
          Please picture me
          In the trees
          I hit my peak at seven feet
          In the swing
          Over the creek
          I was too scared to jump in
          But I, I was high 
          in the sky
          

          almost the same sort of vibe.
