SISTER’S SMILE 10.12.2022

a poem published online in Double Yolk! this stanza is still...strangely cool: 

        I see her friends mindlessly nod
        At the vivid details of her recent travels to a city
        Under lockdown. They praise her volunteerism
        At the dilapidated soup kitchen she’s never visited.
it sort of reminds me of this part of Taylor Swift's song, seven: 
        Please picture me
        In the trees
        I hit my peak at seven feet
        In the swing
        Over the creek
        I was too scared to jump in
        But I, I was high 
        in the sky
